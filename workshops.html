Workshops & Events

Immersive, hands-on workshops where students learn from real professionals — not textbooks. Industry knowledge, practical skills, and lasting networks.

Learning Beyond the Classroom

Freams workshops are designed around one simple belief: the best learning happens when theory meets practice. Each session is crafted with active industry input to ensure every minute of your time is relevant to the real world.

We host both online and in-person workshops — from single-day bootcamps to multi-week series. Every participant leaves with actionable skills, a certificate, and connections that actually open doors.

  • Led by working professionals, not just trainers
  • Certificate of completion recognised by partner companies
  • Small cohorts for maximum interaction
  • Recorded sessions available for review
  • Networking sessions with industry guests
